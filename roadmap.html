📅 CBS Coin – Roadmap (version August 2025)

Overview of milestones and planning.

✅ Q3 2025 – Foundations

  • ✔️ CBS Coin minted on Solana (100,000,000 supply)
  • ✔️ Metadata, logo and IPFS correctly set (manually via Metaboss)
  • ✔️ Initial liquidity pools set up:
    • SOL/CBS pool (2M CBS – 1 SOL)
    • BONK/CBS pool (5M BONK – 1M CBS)
  • ✔️ Public Telegram group created
  • ✔️ Website launched via GitHub Pages
  • ✔️ First CoinGecko submission filed (rejected, resubmission planned)

🔄 Q4 2025 – Growth & Trust

  • 🌱 Gradually add more liquidity on Raydium
  • 👥 Start community building with a focus on trust and transparency
  • 🔥 Experiment with burn mechanisms (e.g., monthly community burns)
  • 🔁 Resubmit to CoinGecko and Token Registry
  • 🧪 First test for a DAO structure or community voting (via Telegram or on-chain)
  • 📊 Keep tokenomics & governance transparent via website and Solscan

🚀 2026 – Community-driven Ecosystem

  • 🗳️ Actively use a DAO for decisions (burns, pool balance, partnerships)
  • 💬 Open contribution model: anyone can contribute to ideas, dev or marketing
  • 🧱 Experiments with CBS utility: microfunding, votes, NFT integrations
  • 🔐 Potential multisig wallet as the community treasury grows
  • 🌀 CBS Coin as a symbol of digital sovereignty & fair ownership
