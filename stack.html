CBS Stack

Free to play • Tap / Click / Space / Enter to drop a block. Perfect stacks glow neon.

How to play

A moving neon block slides horizontally. Tap (or press Space/Enter) to drop it on the stack.

Only the overlapping part stays. Miss entirely → game over.

Perfect alignment (±2px) glows and grants a combo bonus.
